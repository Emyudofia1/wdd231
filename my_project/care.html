Flower Care & Design Tips

Extend the life of your blooms and get inspired to create beautiful arrangements.

🌿 Watering Tips

Change the water every 2 days. Use room temperature water and trim stems at an angle to allow better absorption.

🌞 Light & Temperature

Keep flowers away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Cool, shaded areas help blooms last longer.

💐 Floral Design Basics

Start with greenery, add focal flowers, then filler flowers. Play with height and spacing for a natural look.

🎨 Color Coordination

Use complementary colors or soft gradients. Think about mood — pastels for romance, brights for celebration.
